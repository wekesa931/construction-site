Who we are

For over a decade, CEF Construction has provided high-standard construction and restoration services to clients in the Greater Houston area and all along the Texas and Louisiana Gulf Coast region. As a small construction company that is majority woman-owned, we take pride in how far we've come and the lasting client relationships we've developed. By meeting and exceeding expectations, we've built an industry reputation founded on safety, quality and dependability.

What we do

Concrete Restoration

Secure your structure with our concrete relation services. From surface renovation to full scale structural restoration, we can repair and restor your concrete building and structures so they're just like new again.

New Construction

Build a structural foundation that lasts with our turnkey construction services. starting with initial planning and ite prep all the way through management to completion, we'll care for everything so you don't have to.

Historical Restoration

Certain buildings may require specialized attention during restoration in order to preserve history while still improving foundational integrity. With our focus on quality and precision, count CEF to restore your structure to ots former glory.

Site Maintenance

With regular site maintenance, you can avoid costly total restorations and extend the life of your concrete. Our team will perform routine maintenance and repairs to keep your structure in optimal condition.
